PROJECTS

PROJECT: RAZ.LOG

Action RPG

OCT 2025 — NOW

In “Project Raz.log,” a rebellious engineer emerges from the shadows of a dystopian world: her hammer is as heavy as her rage, her will stronger than any machine. In this third-person action RPG, you are the spark of a rebellion - forged from anger, courage, and steel.

FLORA FORGE

Cozy RTS

APR — JUL 2025

With Flora Forge we bring fresh winds to the RTS Genre by combining the cozy aesthetic and gameplay with strategy and long-term planning. Players help a new robo-plant civilization with the cleanup of their trash planet.

RE:FORMATION

Serious Game

OCT — FEB 2024

"Re:Formation" is a game that highlights the importance and the excitement of being a CyberSecurity Expert. In collaboration with THA_innos we want to inspire you to become a modern day hero and protect yourself, your company and even your country!

GRIMM & GRIMMER

Point & Click Adventure

APR — JUL 2024

A classic point-and-click adventure game inspired by the Grimm fairy tales. It explores the story of two siblings overcoming separation and grief. Targeted at players who love dark and mysterious worlds, the game provides beautiful visuals and a soul-tearing narrative.

REVOLVE

Boardgame Extraction Shooter

Oct - FEB 2024

"Revolve" is a board game that incorporates all the characteristics of a classic extraction shooter. Set in the Wild West, the player must shoot and kill for loot in order to become the strongest headhunter in a post-apocalyptic zombie world. Their trusty revolver will help them on this gruesome path. Designed with passion and creativity, the game features beautiful 3D miniatures and assets, making it a standout in its field.
